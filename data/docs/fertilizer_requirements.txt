GROUNDNUT FERTILIZER REQUIREMENTS - NIGERIA
Source: ICRISAT/IAR Farmer's Guide to Groundnut Production in Nigeria (2015)

Groundnut responds better to residual fertility than direct fertilization.
If a well-fertilized cereal crop preceded groundnut, direct fertilization may not increase yield.

General NPK recommendation for groundnut in Nigeria:
- Nitrogen (N): 25 kg per hectare
- Phosphorus (P2O5): 50 kg per hectare
- Potassium (K2O): 100 kg per hectare

Practical application: Two bags of NPK 15:15:15 plus two bags of Single Super Phosphate (SSP) per hectare.
If groundnut follows a well-fertilized cereal crop, two bags of SSP may be sufficient per hectare.
Apply 400 kg per hectare of gypsum at peak flowering/pegging stage to improve seed filling and oil content.
Conduct soil test first. Liming required only when soil pH is below 5.8.
